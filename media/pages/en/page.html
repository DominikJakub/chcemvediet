What and how?

An ordinary user does not have time to study the law, does not know the exact legal periods and the necessary formalities. We want to enable sharing of the know -how in this area - if someone is able to obtain information of a specific type, it can help and encourage the others to request the same information . It is also possible through the acquired data, which will help inform citizens and reduce the burden on officials .

Through requests for information you can obtain the information that the institution posseses . You do not need to justify the reason  -- anyone can request the information. However, it is not possible to obtain

  • other people's personal information , or confidential information - these are censored out from the institution's answer
  • the institution is not required to "produce" the information, only look for it. It is, for example, not obliged to prepare an analysis if such an analysis does not exist
  • do not use this for complaints - it is a different proces (according to a different law)

You will need:

  • To describe what you are asking for. This can be a description, such as "Leasing contract of the object at 1 Ruzova St. "
  • If you know the exact document number, it is good to put it into the application (but it is not obligatory).
  • To know, who are you requesting the information from.
  • It is necessary to provide your "snail" mail address: In case of a negative decision, the decision is always sent in a paper.

You do not need:

  • a digital signature/certificate
  • any specific form
